TN14 MI Charger

Fault Description

Solutions
Charger Model
LED Indicator

Check charger LED:

  • Traffic light (red-orange-green): System reset. Occurs at power on and battery connection
  • Slow Orange blink: System waiting. Battery Disconnected
  • Solid Orange: Constant current phase.
  • Orange with Green blink: Constant voltage phase
  • Solid Green: Charge Complete
Battery charger not charging

Check charger LED:

  • Three Red Flashes: Charge suspended. Battery volts too low.
  • Two Red Flashes: Charge suspended. Battery volts too high.
  • Slow Red blinking (1 flash every 5 sec): Charge suspended. Battery too hot. Or PCB too hot (PCB Self protected to 75`C)
  • Fast Red blinking: Thermistor Error. (Needs Power Reset) l Orange blinking (1 flash every ½ sec): Timeout. Charger is disconnected, plug into TN14
  • Solid Red: Fault. (Needs Power Reset)
Orange blinking (1 flash every ½ sec): even when plugged into the TN14. Power off TN14 during the charge cycle. If the issue persists, contact

Getac

Fault Description

Solutions

Tablet does not turn on.

 

(Battery Indicator)

Recharge using supplied charger until the battery indicator turns from Red to at least Orange, Green means tablet is fully charged.

List of indicators:

  • Orange glow indicates that battery charging is in progress.
  • Orange flashes indicates that the current battery level is insufficient for starting up the device. Wait till the flashing light turns into solid light before you try to start up the device.
  • Red and Orange flashes in turn indicates that the battery is in an abnormal state. Contact in case this happens.
  • Red glow indicates that the battery power is critically low (less than 10%).

  • Red flashes inform you of scheduled appointments, alarms and reminders

  • Green glow indicates that the battery is fully charged
If indicator is “Orange” or “Green” and unit does not seem to switch on, press and hold the power button for at least 10 seconds. The tablet should now start up.
If tablet does not turn on, hold the power button for 10 seconds.
If tablet fails to reboot and not responding at all, contact .
Tablet is not responding, frozen. Hold power button for 10 seconds, until it shuts down, then turn on again.
Tablet displays "Password expired". Contact the senior geologist or REFLEXHUB- IQ administrator to reset your password.
Tablet displays "Invalid credential" Check your username and password and try again. Failing this, contact the REFLEXHUB- IQ administrator to reset your password.
