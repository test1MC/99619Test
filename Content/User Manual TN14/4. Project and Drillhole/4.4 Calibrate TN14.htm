Calibrate TN14

The is calibrated every time it is powered on.

The cannot be calibrated unless a drillhole is created or selected first. See Add Drillhole on page 1.

The Drillhole Name cannot be edited once it is initialised.

Calibration takes approximately 12 minutes.

Do not move the while it is calibrating.

To Calibrate the

  1. Select a Project.

 

  1. Select a Drillhole.

 

  1. Tap Survey on the tablet.

 

Calibration begins.

Once calibration is complete, the displays the Azimuth and Dip screen, and is now ready to perform the survey.

See Survey on page 1 for an explanation of the fields.
