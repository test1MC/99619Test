Get Location

Get Location allows the tablet to automatically determine the latitude and longitude co-ordinates by GPS.

  1. Select the Get Location button.

The GPS commences the search for the latitude and longitude co-ordinates.

Co-ordinates display including the Accuracy, shown as amber in colour as it is outside the set tolerance of 4 metres.

The Accuracy tolerance is user defined, see Settings on page 1.

The Accuracy lowers as it approaches closer to the tolerance set value.

  1. Once the co-ordinates are within tolerance, the Accuracy indicates the value by the colour green. Select Accept.

The longitude and latitude co-ordinates are set.

While waiting for the GPS to obtain co-ordinates, other fields such as the Dip and Azimuth can be set.

 

Ensure to use Get Location in view of a clear sky, as the GPS may be unable to fix a position.
