There are two types of surveys, both operated the same way:

Rig Alignment: The drill rig is aligned to achieve accurate azimuth and dip values based on planned values prior to drilling

Collar Capture: The TN14 captures the Azimuth and Dip values of an existing drillhole

 

 

Operate Rig
   
   

 

Monitor the tablet while the rig is moved:

  • Rig Alignment: Move the rig to align the planned Azimuth and Dip
  • Collar Capture: Move the rig to ascertain the existing Azimuth and Dip
