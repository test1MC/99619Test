Charge TN14

The battery requires charging on a regular basis before using the system.

Only use the provided charger for the battery, using another charger will damage the battery cells. The battery uses a tri-axial connector.

To check battery power:

The battery is internally sealed into the . The has an LED indicator. Pressing the button reveals the status of the battery. If all LEDs are on the battery is full.

Do not use the if:
• Power button is Red
__
• First LED is flashing
In either instance, the requires charging.

 

To charge the battery:

  1. Remove the from the case.
  2. Connect the Smart Charger 2A (Part No. 103730) to mains.

Refer toTN14 Kit on page 1 to identify parts.

  1. Connect the Smart Charger 2A (Part No. 103730) to the ensuring PINS match.

  1. The Smart Charger 2A LED indicators:
    • Red Solid: Battery is charging
    • Green Solid: Battery is full
    • Green Flash Slowly: The battery is not connected to the charger
    • Red Flashing: Battery is damaged, or voltage is below 6V

LED will be off if short circuit or polarity is reversed.
