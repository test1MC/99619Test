Attach Instrument

The is a complete unit that is attached to a rod or barrel of a drill rig, or to an existing drill rod or pipe.

Before attaching the to the rig, calibrate the . See Calibrate TN14 on page 1.


How to attach TN14

  1. Lift the by the handles from the case.

Do not lift or carry the by the clamp.

 

  1. Place the onto the rod of the rig, ensuring the clamp is open and fits over the rod.

  1. While holding the TN14, pull out the wheel lever.

  1. Turn the wheel clockwise until the clamp tightens to rod.
