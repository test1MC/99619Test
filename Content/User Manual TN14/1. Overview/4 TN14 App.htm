TN14 App

The App is the software program used on the tablet to manage surveys. In summary, it performs the following functions:

  • Connects to the (cloud based system)
  • Calibrates the
  • Add projects and drillholes
  • Verifies and displays rig alignment
  • Transfers survey data





Launch Application

  1. Turn on the tablet by pressing the Power button.
  2. On the tablet screen, swipe the screen, and select the Padlock icon.

 

The Desktop displays.

  1. Tap the GYROCOMPASS icon.
  2. The Splash page displays, followed by the Home screen.

 

If the Splash page does not display, the is already running.

Re-Start Application

There is no exit option on the . The application remains idle when not in use.

Only use this option if the is not responding.

  1. Tap the Show Running Apps icon.

 

  1. Swipe the app to the left to close.

Once closed, the app no longer display in Show Running Apps.

Do not close the while data is being downloaded.

  1. On the desktop, tap the to restart the program.
