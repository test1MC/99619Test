Overview

The REFLEX TN14 GYROCOMPASS improves productivity dramatically by quickly and accurately aligning drill rigs in surface and underground operations. The REFLEX TN14 GYROCOMPASS takes approximately 10 minutes to complete its self calibration and a drill rig can then be aligned to the correct azimuth and dip within minutes. The unit is supplied with a single internal battery. With a dip range of +/- 90°, the REFLEX TN14 GYROCOMPASS has the flexibility to improve productivity in all drilling conditions. Direct data communication with the secure, cloud based REFLEXHUB-IQ allows efficient drill program management in near real time, from anywhere.

GYROCOMPASS operates in either True North mode or Mine Grid mode.
