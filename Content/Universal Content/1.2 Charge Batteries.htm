Safety and Warnings

Avoid contact of liquid from leaking battery or cell with skin or eyes. In any event, wash area with water repetitively and seek medical advice.

Only use the provided chargers for charging instruments and the third-party devices or components.

Only use the provided or recommended batteries for Instruments and third-party supplied devices and components.

Do not expose batteries to heat or fire.

Do not dissemble batteries, and mix brands, capacities or sizes.

Ensure batteries are charged indoors.

Store batteries as instructed in this manual and with supplied placard in the pelican case. Beware of storing in direct sunlight, short-circuited with other metal objects.

Ensure batteries are kept clean and dry.

Ensure battery terminals are wiped with a clean dry cloth.

Once a battery is fully charged, avoid prolonged charging when not in use.

Remove the battery from the instrument when not in use.
