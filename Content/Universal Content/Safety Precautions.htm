Safety Precautions

This manual contains important information to ensure the safe and effective use of this system. Please read this information carefully.

Warnings and Cautions

Warnings and cautions are used to highlight potential hazards. Failure to observe warnings and cautions as highlighted in this information may result in injury or property damage.

Ensure your understanding of each before proceeding.

 

WARNING: Indicates a potentially lethal hazard. Failure to observe a WARNING may result in severe injury or death.

 

CAUTION: Failure to observe a CAUTION may result in damage to the device or other property.

 

Notes and Tips

Notes provide extra information relative to the current topic.

 

Tips offer examples, reminders or other helpful pointers and positioned so as to be read before commencing a topic or step.
